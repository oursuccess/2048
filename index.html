How to play:Use your arrow keys to move the tiles.Merge them into one!

Bad Cells:惩罚方块:

⛔:Unmovable. cannot add with other cells(can be remove by powerups).无法移动，无法与其它方块合并（但可以被奖励方块移除）

❄:Movable. cannot add with other cells(can be remove by powerups).可以移动，但无法与其它方块合并

3:Moveable. can add with other 3s(can be removed by powerups).可以移动，可以和3结合（虽然并没有什么用）

PowerUps(may not so good): （并不一定好的）奖励方块

✂: remove one cell in the direction player choose.移除玩家行进方向上的一个方块

📩: remove the whole column or row of the cell, direction is on player's choice, and change to these nums' sum.将本行/列所有方块移除，并变为这些方块之和（向2取整，若为空则变为坏方块）

➖: remove the whole column and row of this cell, change to these num's sum.移除本行与本列所有方块，并返回这些方块的和（向2的N次方取整）

➕: can sum to any cell.变为移动方向上的方块对应的数（若不存在或其无法合并，则不变化）

🔀: sum cells with specific num on the gird, and change to these nums' sum.消除特定数字，然后在本方块原地生成这些数字的和（向2的N次方取整）

🔁: change to a random num(range in (1 ~ double of the max value on the grid).变为一个随机的数字（2~场上最大数字的2倍）

💣: a great bomb that can remove all cells on the 3*3 grids of this cell, and change to these cells' sum(floor to power of 2).消除自身为中心3*3的所有方块，然后变为这些方块的和
