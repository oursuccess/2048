How to play:Use your arrow keys to move the tiles.Merge them into one!

Bad Cells::

1:Unmovable. cannot add with other cells(can be remove by powerups).

3:Moveable. cannot add with other cells(can be removed by powerups).

PowerUps(may not so good):

7: remove one cell in the direction player choose.

77: remove the whole column or row of the cell, direction is on player's choice, and change to these nums' sum(around those cell's sum, the same to 777 and 999).

777: remove the whole column and row of this cell, change to these num's sum.

9: can sum to any cell(to 1,3 and power of 2s).

233: sum cells with specific num on the gird, and change to these nums' sum.

666: change to a random num(range in (1 ~ double of the max value on the grid).

999: a great bomb that can remove all cells on the 3*3 grids of this cell, and change to these cells' sum(floor to power of 2).

after won or your scoe is above 2048, there will be other powerup cells and trouble cells.
